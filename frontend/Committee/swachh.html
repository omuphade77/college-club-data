SWACHH VJTI

Swachh VJTI was launched by passionate students to foster a cleaner, greener, and healthier environment inside and outside the college premises.

Poster Making Competition

On the occasion of National Pollution Control Day, we organized a poster making competition to spread awareness among youth about pollution and the importance of immediate preventive measures.

Activities Conducted

Donation Drive

At the start of the year, Swachh VJTI launched a successful donation drive with participation from students and faculty. The initiative received overwhelming support from the entire student body.

Campus Cleanup

A large-scale campus cleanup drive was conducted with students, teaching staff, and non-teaching staff. Over 100+ participants contributed to making the campus cleaner.

Cleanup Drive with BMC

In collaboration with BMC, Swachh VJTI organized a drive near Five Gardens and Matunga to spread awareness about cleanliness with active participation from students and faculty.
